About us

Warm recipes, honest ingredients, friendly service.

Sweet Crumbs Bakery is a modern local bakery created for students, office workers, families, local residents, and tourists who want fresh baked goods in a welcoming place.

Our baking philosophy is simple: use quality ingredients, prepare each item with care, and make every visit feel easy, comforting, and memorable.
